What is this?: This is an topic-annotated and backlinked overlay to The Uncertainty Mindset, a self-therapeutic newsletter project by me, Vaughn Tan.

How to use this: In my view, the best way to navigate the 52 issues of the newsletter is by topic. However, you could also look at this list of issues; each link there takes you to a issue summary from which—if you are that way inclined—you can find the original newsletter issue on Substack.

Also: I wrote a book about the uncertainty mindset, which is confusingly also titled The Uncertainty Mindset, and which I hope you will consider purchasing.
