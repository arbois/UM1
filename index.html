What is this?: It's an topic-annotated and bidirectionally linked overlay to The Uncertainty Mindset, a year-long self-therapeutic newsletter project by me, Vaughn Tan. The newsletter was a forcing function for me to write about how uncertainty influences everything from the food we eat to the way businesses are organized, and to develop strategies for coping with and benefiting from uncertainty. The newsletter itself is hosted by Substack, and you can find it here.

How to use this:

  1. In my view, the best way to navigate the 52 issues of the newsletter is by topic. Some of my favorites are resource with no predefined form, affordances, productive discomfort, surprise and delight, and pizza. Here is the full list of topics.
  2. However, you could also look at this list of issues.
  3. The sidebar (or top bar, if you're on a mobile device) always shows a link to return to this index page if you become hopelessly disoriented.
