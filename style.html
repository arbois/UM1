style

Vaughn Tan3rd January 2021 at 4:04pm

The recognizable and consistent mark of the maker (which can be an individual or an organization in a product (which of course includes services, processes, and ways of thinking). A recognizable and consistent style is especially difficult to combine with innovation, which is of course when it is most strategically valuable.
