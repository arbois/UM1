diversity

Vaughn Tan3rd January 2021 at 11:51am

Often defined as difference in terms of a predefined set of categories—but a better way to think about diversity in terms of multidimensional and multilevel difference. True diversity is how rich a system is in different types of information, no matter what form those differences take and what carries that information.
