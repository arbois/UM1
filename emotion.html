emotion

Vaughn Tan3rd January 2021 at 12:29pm

Broadly, non-cognitive responses to things and situations—the individual and organizational response to uncertainty is often instinctive, visceral, and emotional avoidance or fear. But emotion is also the basis of surprise and delight, which can be quite nice and is always the product of uncertainty.
