sustainability

Vaughn Tan3rd January 2021 at 3:33pm

The ability of a process or system to persist, ideally indefinitely. One often-overlooked aspect of sustainability is innovation. In this newsletter, sustainability is often not far in the background in discussions of product, design, the food industry, restaurants, low-intervention wine, and regenerative agriculture.
