restaurants

Vaughn Tan3rd January 2021 at 5:15pm

Places where you pay someone else to make food for you. We love them. My book about the uncertainty mindset is based on quite a lot of research with some of the best restaurants and culinary R&D teams in the world. In the newsletter, I often use examples from restaurants to illustrate ideas, so they show up a lot.
