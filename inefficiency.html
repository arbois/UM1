inefficiency

Vaughn Tan3rd January 2021 at 1:27pm

When things seem wasteful or insufficiently optimised for a particular situation. In this newsletter, I often argue that being ready for uncertainty entails having resources that are held in reserve for unexpected deployment—inefficiency is thus a necessary tradeoff when preparing for uncertain futures.
