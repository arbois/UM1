resource with no predefined form

Vaughn Tan3rd January 2021 at 5:25pm

Resources are often thought of as things which have some kind of concrete form or predefined function. In contrast, a resource with no predefined form crystallises into a specific form and function at the point of need. These kinds of resources are particularly strategic when faced with uncertain futures.
