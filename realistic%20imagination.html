realistic imagination

Vaughn Tan3rd January 2021 at 5:54pm

The ability to imagine something that doesn't exist yet, but in a way that is grounded in existing reality. This is essential for innovation, for adaptability in times of uncertainty, and is an important part of developing the uncertainty mindset. Seems like a contradiction but isn't.
